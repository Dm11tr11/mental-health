What Is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act, especially in teens who are going through many emotional and physical changes.

Self-Care Tips

  • Maintain a balanced diet
  • Get enough sleep and rest
  • Practice mindfulness and relaxation techniques
  • Stay physically active
  • Talk to someone you trust
